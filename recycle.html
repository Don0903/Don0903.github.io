Benefits of buying second hand




When considering replacing their phone, people are usually interested in the new model just released, since it is expected to be more functional and useful. However, why not buy a second hand product? The second hand mobile phones market is expected to reach 65 billion dollars in market value in 2024, since more and more people are becoming aware of this option.

On average, in Europe, mobile phones are kept for 1 year and a half before being thrown away, whereas their lifespan could reach 5 years and up to 10 years if fixed when needed. Knowing where to look, it is possible and advantageous to buy a second-hand phone of high-quality and at an affordable price.
